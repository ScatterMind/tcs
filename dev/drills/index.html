          Symbols used in the drills

          Drills use placeholder symbols, not the shop's actual numbers. Look these up live or from shop policy before quoting.

          MID
          Live mid-market BTC/CAD price from the shop's reference feed.
          ASK
          Shop's sell-to-customer price. ASK = MID × (1 + s) where s is the shop spread on the buy-side.
          BID
          Shop's buy-from-customer price. BID = MID × (1 − s) where s is the shop spread on the sell-side.
          s
          Shop spread (% over/under mid). Set per shop policy — not encoded here.
          Quote validity
          How long a price quote stays good. Per shop policy.
          Confirmations
          How many on-chain confirmations to wait for before paying out cash on a BTC→cash deposit. Per shop policy.

          Compliance lines referenced

          $1,000 ID line
          Identity verification required at/above this amount per virtual-currency exchange transaction. General FINTRAC — not shop-specific.
          $10,000 report line
          Large-transaction reporting threshold. Same-person, same-type transactions aggregate over a 24-hour window — splitting doesn't avoid it.
          STR
          Suspicious Transaction Report. No dollar minimum; required on reasonable grounds to suspect, including attempted transactions. Tipping off the customer is prohibited.

          What this covers

          Two drill types: trade mechanics (direction × which side the customer pins, across four CAD tiers, randomized amounts) and red flags (currently structuring / smurfing — recognizing and correctly handling attempts to split under the ID and reporting lines). USDT and other-coin drills are planned. Customer-side red flags like scam-victim and counterfeit remain a separate set.
