        Decision flow for any trade-mechanics interaction. Edge cases (stale quote, mid-trade price move, round-number ask) are notes off the main flow, not separate branches.

        Symbols used in the drills

        Drills use placeholder symbols, not the shop's actual numbers. Look these up live or from shop policy before quoting.

        MID
        Live mid-market BTC/CAD price from the shop's reference feed.
        ASK
        Shop's sell-to-customer price. ASK = MID × (1 + s) where s is the shop spread on the buy-side.
        BID
        Shop's buy-from-customer price. BID = MID × (1 − s) where s is the shop spread on the sell-side.
        s
        Shop spread (% over/under mid). Set per shop policy — not encoded here.
        Quote validity
        How long a price quote stays good. Per shop policy.
        Confirmations
        How many on-chain confirmations to wait for before paying out cash on a BTC→cash deposit. Per shop policy.

        What this drill covers

        Trade mechanics only (direction, constraint side, fee framing, quote handling, round-number asks). FINTRAC/AML and customer-side red flags are separate drill sets — not in this cut.
